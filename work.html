Game Design & Development
ABOUT
I'm interested in exploring one simple question: what can games do that other artistic mediums can't?
I focus my investigation by making and playing puzzle games and walking simulators. I'm an experienced Unity dev and like to dabble in Unreal and Bevy.
I spend my free time rockclimbing, studying Haskell and Rust, and playing chess.
CURRENTLY
Lead Game Dev at GamExPro making VR escape games.
